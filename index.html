properly implemented to revolutionize and disrupt how data, values, assets, contracts are interacted and transacted without the need for third parties.

About Us

"With e-currency based on cryptographic proof, without the need to trust a third party middleman, money can be secure and transactions effortless." - Satoshi Nakamoto

Indonesian Blockchain Network coordinates the efforts of the blockchain community, to promote and create awareness of blockchain technology, to educate members on development level, to advocate blockchain technology implementation to technologists and regulators, and to contribute to decentralization projects globally.

OBJECTIVES

Raise awareness for blockchain technology and crypto-related projects

Organize blockchain/crypto-related meetups

Administer online blockchain related contents

Organize blockchain/crypto-related technical workshops

Fund Indonesia Blockchain Network through supports from blockchain related companies
